About SeeingPower

Industrial supply built on chemical insight and export execution.

SeeingPower is the international business brand of Taizhou ShengLing Technology Co., Ltd., supporting overseas customers with organic peroxides, industrial materials, Cummins & Perkins power systems and practical sourcing coordination.

Power running. Materials flowing.

SeeingPower was created for customers who need more than a simple product list. In organic peroxides and industrial supply, buyers care about stable supply, application understanding, clear communication and reliable delivery. Our work is to connect these pieces into one practical supply solution.

Our company has invested in and cooperates with selected chemical-industry enterprises, which gives us closer access to production-side knowledge and a deeper understanding of organic peroxide applications. This background helps us communicate more clearly with customers in FRP, composites, polymers and manufacturing fields.

Alongside industrial materials, SeeingPower also supports Cummins & Perkins power systems and solar power projects, giving global customers a wider sourcing window for industrial growth.
